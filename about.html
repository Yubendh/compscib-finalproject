About Wat2Watch

Welcome to Wat2Watch, a movie recommendation for movie and tv lovers who spend too much time looking and not actually watching. Although this is still a barebones website, we plan to improve it even further in the near future!

Powered by the OMDb API, we look through thousands of titles and bring you a curated list of shows that match your preferences through criteria search.

How It Works

Wat2Watch gives you control so that you can forget about the hassle. Use our specific criteria filters to specify the exact year, genre, and quality rating you desire.

The Tech Stack

This website was built using modern web technologies including JavaScript for high performance, standard CSS3 for responsive design, and has api intergration for real-time data of what you search for.
